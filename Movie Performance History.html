Welcome

Understanding customer preferences and desires is a necessity for maintaining and growing any successful business. In the film industry, it is particularly important for directors to have a clear and thorough understanding of successful movie qualities to provide highly sought after films to maximize viewership.
This program utilizes Blockbuster Movie and IMDbPro Domestic Box Office Performance data to analyze movie performance based on common movie-specific characteristics.
The pages of this Web application display the data tables constructed within our database, and present analyses and insights to the film industry through several forms of visualization and evaluation in the context of historical movie data.
